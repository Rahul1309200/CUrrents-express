About Panache

Panache is the modeling club at Chitkara University. We bring together students passionate about fashion, design, and photography, creating an inclusive space for aspiring models to learn, grow, and showcase their talent. Our mission is to foster creativity, confidence, and collaboration through regular events and workshops.
